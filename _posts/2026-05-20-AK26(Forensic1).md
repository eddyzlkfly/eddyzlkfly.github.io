## **"Hidden Layers"**

During an internal audit, a seemingly harmless image was discovered on a workstation.

Nothing unusual at first glance… just a logo.

But something feels off, almost like there's more beneath the surface.

Can you uncover what’s really hidden inside?

**Hint 1:** Sometimes, files are not what they seem.
**Hint 2:** Dig deeper into the file… there might be something embedded.
**Hint 3:** Metadata can reveal more than you think.
